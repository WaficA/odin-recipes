Honeyed Ham Glaze

Description

This ham glaze recipe is gently spiced, and sweetened with honey. Not too spiced or too sweet.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried minced onion
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground ginger

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix together the honey and ketchup
  2. Add the mustard
  3. Add the Worcestershire sauce
  4. Add the dried minced onion
  5. Add the ground cloves
  6. Then the ginger
  7. Pour over ham slices as a glaze
