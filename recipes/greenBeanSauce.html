Green Bean Sauce

Description

Flavorful sauce that can be put on green beans, asparagus, broccoli, etc.

Ingredients

  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • ½ teaspoon prepared horseradish
  • ½ teaspoon lemon juice
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch dried parsley, or to taste
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs with mayonnaise
  2. Add horseradish and lemon juice
  3. Add the salt, black pepper, garlic powder
  4. Add the parsley, and Worcestershire sauce
  5. Mix the Ingredients until well blend
